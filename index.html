About

I am a researcher focusing on LLM post-training — supervised fine-tuning, reinforcement learning, and reward modeling for aligning large language models with downstream tasks. I am especially interested in agentic systems, and have built and trained both search agents and code agents.

I am currently a PhD student at the Singapore University of Technology and Design (SUTD), and a research intern at MiroMind. I received my Master's degree from Shanghai Jiao Tong University (SJTU) and my Bachelor's degree from Tianjin University.

I believe the simplest methods that scale tend to be the ones that last.

Feel free to reach out at xiao_yao@mymail.sutd.edu.sg.
